DEFECT REPORT — Sprint 24 · Penjaminan Cash Loan / Jamkrindo

Title:
  ICPR (Indonesia Credit Penjaminan Repository) Sertifikat Penjaminan
  upload times out, dropping certificates from the regulatory submission
  batch — OJK 7-day reporting SLA at risk.

Reporter   : Budi Santoso (QC Analyst)
Reported   : 2026-04-13 09:14 WIB
Detected by: ETL Pipeline Validator (PJM-RUN-20260413-001)
Environment: PROD-LIKE / Sprint 24 release candidate

Regulatory context:
  Per OJK POJK 1/POJK.05/2016 (jo. POJK 11/2025), every Sertifikat
  Penjaminan issued by Jamkrindo MUST be uploaded to ICPR within 7
  working days of issuance. Missing uploads carry an OJK administrative
  penalty (Pasal 27) and risk losing the LJK (Lembaga Jasa Keuangan)
  rating used by partner banks (Mandiri, BRI, BNI, BTN) when allocating
  guarantee quota to Jamkrindo.

Summary:
  The 06_icpr_upload_sertifikat stage of the penjaminan_submission
  pipeline is failing with HTTP 504 (gateway timeout) when calling
  api.icpr.ojk.go.id. Today's run lost 6 of 18 certificates (-33.3%),
  silently dropping high-value guarantees from the OJK report:
    CIF-2026-00011 (Korporat, plafon Rp 2 B)
    CIF-2026-00014 (Reguler,  plafon Rp 850 jt)
    CIF-2026-00020 (Reguler,  plafon Rp 950 jt)
    CIF-2026-00024 (Korporat, plafon Rp 1.2 B)
    CIF-2026-00006 (Reguler,  plafon Rp 750 jt)
    CIF-2026-00010 (Reguler,  plafon Rp 600 jt)

Severity:
  HIGH — regulatory + financial integrity. Total exposure of dropped
  certs = Rp 6.95 B (~26% of sprint plafon). If not uploaded by
  2026-04-22 (7 working days from issuance), Jamkrindo will be in
  breach of OJK reporting requirement.

Steps to reproduce:
  1. Run pipeline=penjaminan_submission for sprint 24 cohort
  2. Stage 06 ICPR upload hits 30s timeout for 6 records
  3. Pipeline marks status=PARTIAL_SUCCESS (misleading — should FAIL)
  4. ICPR confirmation IDs missing for the 6 certs
  5. Internal sertifikat_status remains 'PENDING_ICPR' indefinitely

Expected:
  - ICPR upload succeeds with confirmation ID (ICPR-2026-XXXXXXXX), OR
  - Failed records are retried with exponential backoff, OR
  - Failed records routed to DLQ with PagerDuty alert to Compliance
  - Pipeline final status = FAIL, not PARTIAL_SUCCESS

Actual:
  - 6 certificates silently dropped, no alert
  - sertifikat_status stays 'PENDING_ICPR' with no auto-retry
  - Compliance Officer has no visibility until manual reconciliation

Pattern history (cross-sprint, same root cause):
  Sprint 22 (2026-03-30) : ICPR timeout, 1 cert dropped  [DEF-2026-0120]
  Sprint 23 (2026-04-04) : ICPR timeout, 1 cert dropped  [DEF-2026-0122]
  Sprint 23 (2026-04-08) : ICPR timeout, 2 certs dropped [DEF-2026-0125]
  Sprint 24 (2026-04-13) : ICPR timeout, 6 certs dropped [THIS DEFECT]
  -> Trend escalating 1 → 1 → 2 → 6. ICPR endpoint degrading or our
     payload size growing (cert PDFs now embed e-materai).

Recommended fix (Definition of Done):
  1. Wrap ICPR client in retry policy: 3 attempts, exponential backoff
     (1s, 4s, 16s), total budget 90s (covers ICPR p95)
  2. Add circuit breaker — open at 50% failure rate over 5-min window
  3. Failed sertifikat → dead-letter queue (Kafka topic
     jamkrindo.icpr.dlq), Compliance Officer dashboard polls the DLQ
  4. PagerDuty alert if DLQ depth > 0 at end of run
  5. Pipeline final status = FAIL (not PARTIAL_SUCCESS) when any cert
     reaches DLQ — forces explicit Compliance ack
  6. Compress cert PDFs >2 MB before upload (current avg = 3.2 MB
     including embedded e-materai)
  7. Add OJK 7-day countdown indicator on Compliance dashboard for
     every cert in PENDING_ICPR state
